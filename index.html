Pro Tips for Success

Master these techniques to become the ultimate street racing legend

🏎️ Master the Drift

Use the handbrake while turning to initiate controlled drifts around corners. Practice timing your drift entries and exits for maximum speed retention.

🚦 Know Your Routes

Learn the city layout and identify escape routes, shortcuts, and hiding spots. Knowledge of the terrain is crucial for successful evasion.

⚡ Strategic Nitro Use

Save your nitro boosts for critical moments - escaping tight situations, reaching top speeds on highways, or making crucial overtakes.

🎯 Avoid Damage

Minimize collisions with obstacles and other vehicles. A damaged car performs poorly, making it harder to escape pursuit situations.
